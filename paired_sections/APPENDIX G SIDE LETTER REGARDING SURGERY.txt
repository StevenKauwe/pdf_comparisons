
# Sunrise
APPENDIX G 

SIDE LETTER REGARDING SURGERY 
In Surgical Services, the Hospital shall make every effort to accommodate the request of 
an employee who has worked for sixteen (16) hours within the last twenty-four (24) hours to 
have at least ten (10) hours of rest before having to return to work as part of the regular schedule. 
An employee may choose to use PTO for such time as the employee is excused from work on the 
succeeding shift. 
In the event this language presents operational or implementation problems, the parties 
shall meet to attempt to resolve these issues.
