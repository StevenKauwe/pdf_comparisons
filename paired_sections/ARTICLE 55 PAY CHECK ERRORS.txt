
# Sunrise
ARTICLE 55 

PAY CHECK ERRORS 
55.1 
Employer errors resulting in underpayments to employees shall be corrected and an 
additional check for the underpayment shall be issued to the employee within three weekdays 
(excluding holidays). 
55.2 
Employee errors will be corrected as soon as possible but no later than the next paycheck 
distributed five (5) or more days after submission of the payroll discrepancy form.
