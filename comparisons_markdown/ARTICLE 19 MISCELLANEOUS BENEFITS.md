### Summary of Sunrise Section

**ARTICLE 19: MISCELLANEOUS BENEFITS**

1. **General Agreement**: Benefits and programs will remain in effect without revision or modification except as specified.
2. **Free Parking**: Hospital provides free parking for employees. Any changes to parking location will be bargained.
3. **Cafeteria Discounts**: Employees receive free coffee and at least a 20% discount off visitors' prices in the cafeteria.
4. **Hospital Services Discounts**: Existing employee discount for hospital services at Sunrise Hospital will continue.
5. **Parent Company Benefits**: Various benefits offered through the Hospital’s parent company will continue on the same basis as for other hourly employees. Changes to these programs are not subject to grievance and arbitration but will be notified 60 days in advance, and effects will be bargained.

**Parent Company Benefits Include**:
- Wells Fargo Financial Services and Education Program
- Day Care Spending Account
- Medical/Vision Spending Account
- HCA Scholarship Program
- Adoption Assistance
- Direct Deposit
- Student Loan Repayment Program
- Tuition Reimbursement Program
- Child Care Subsidy
- Employee Stock Purchase Plan
