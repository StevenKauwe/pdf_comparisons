### Summary of Sunrise Section

**ARTICLE 27: WEEKEND AND SHIFT DIFFERENTIALS**

- **27.1 Evening Shift Differential:** 
  - Eligibility: At least 4 consecutive hours of a shift scheduled for 3:00 p.m. – 11:00 p.m.
  - Rate: $1.50 per hour
  - Applies only to productive hours worked.

- **27.2 Night Shift Differential:** 
  - Eligibility: At least 4 consecutive hours of a shift scheduled for 7:00 p.m. – 7:00 a.m. or 11:00 p.m. – 7:00 a.m.
  - Rate: $2.10 per hour for regular full-time or part-time employees, $1.50 per hour for VSP employees
  - Applies only to productive hours worked.

- **27.3 Weekend Shift Differential:** 
  - Eligibility: At least 4 consecutive hours of a shift scheduled to commence on or after 11:00 p.m. Friday and end on or before 11:00 p.m. Sunday.
  - Rate: $1.50 per hour
  - Applies only to productive hours worked.

- **27.4 Eligibility For Differentials:** 
  - Employees will not be ineligible for differentials if called off by the Hospital due to lack of work.
  - Hours on a shift worked prior to and after a meal period are considered consecutive hours.

- **27.5 No Pyramiding:** 
  - No pyramiding of differentials except:
    1. Evening Shift Differential and Night Shift Differential will be paid in combination with Weekend Shift Differential.
    2. Evening Shift Differential, Night Shift Differential, and Weekend Shift Differential will be paid in combination with applicable holiday differentials.
