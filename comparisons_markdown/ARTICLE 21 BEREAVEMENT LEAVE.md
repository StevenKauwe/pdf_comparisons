### Summary of Sunrise Section

**ARTICLE 21: BEREAVEMENT LEAVE**

- **Benefit**: 
  - Full-time employees: Up to 24 hours of paid leave.
  - Part-time employees: Up to 12 hours of paid leave.
  - Time off must be taken within 30 days following the date of death.
  
- **Immediate Family Definition**: 
  - Spouse, parent, step-parent, legal guardian, grandparents, grandchildren, mother-in-law, father-in-law, children, step-children, legal ward, brother (including in-law), sister (including in-law), son-in-law, daughter-in-law, domestic partner (as per Nevada Revised Statutes 122A.100), and such domestic partner’s relatives.
  - Includes bereavement leave for parents experiencing a miscarriage, stillbirth, or similar outcome of a fetus.
  
- **Bereavement Pay**: 
  - At base hourly wage with no premium pay.
  - Not counted as hours worked.
  
- **Additional Days**: 
  - Employees may request additional days of bereavement leave.
  - If PTO is available, it must be utilized for these additional days.

### Differences and Recommendations

- **Differences**: There are no significant differences between the bereavement leave policies of Sunrise, Mountain View, and Southern Hills. All three contracts provide the same benefits, definitions, and conditions for bereavement leave.

- **Recommendations**: Since there are no differences, Sunrise does not need to adopt any changes from Mountain View or Southern Hills. The current language in the Sunrise contract is already aligned with the other two contracts and is comprehensive.
